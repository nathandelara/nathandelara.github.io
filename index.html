About Me

I am currently on long-term leave from the French administration, working as a quantitative researcher for a hedgefund in Paris.
My job consists in applying machine learning techniques to various trading strategies, especially for commodities future contracts.
Before this, I had a few different lives that I invite you to discover in this page.

Civil servant

I began my career as engineer at the the French Direction Générale de l'Armement, working on sky surveillance programs.


But, at the peak of the COVID crisis, I was appointed deputy prefect in the departement of Calvados, Normandy by president Emmanuel Macron.
Beside from the usual security duties, my mission was to deploy emergency economic policies in order to protect employment while laying ground for the ecological transition and an industrial rebound.


I continued to work for the French economy from the Directorate for Enterprises in Paris, leading a team dedicated to innovation policies.
My job was to coordinate the national investment plan France 2030, with special focus on the Venture Capital funds.

Scientist

After studying machine learning during my graduate studies, I discovered the wonderful world of graph mining while interning at the Deparment of Defence.
I decided to pursue a PhD in this very field under the direction of Thomas Bonald.
This has given me the opportunity to publish several papers in conferences, workshops and scientific journals, besides working on an exciting open-source project!

Military training

My military journey began when I entered the École polytechnique as a cadet.
After an initial training in La Courtine camp, I chose to intern in the Air Force in order to fulfill my dream of flying on a jet.
Later on, I went to paratrooper school in Pau, to get my airborn certification.
For my last year before graduation, I had the opportunity to lead a platoon of new cadets during their initial training.

This is when I decided to enter a full military career as an Armament Engineer.
Complementary training included four months in the 1st Airborn Marines special forces regiment in Bayonne and one month of navigation on the Mistral ship.

As of 2022, I hold the rank of Principal Engineer (Major).
